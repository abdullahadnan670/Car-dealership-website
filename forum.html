Frequently Asked Questions

Small cars like the Honda Civic, Toyota Corolla, or Mini Cooper are great for city driving due to their size and fuel efficiency.

Regular oil changes, checking the cooling system, and replacing the air filter are essential for engine maintenance.

Driving at steady speeds, maintaining proper tire pressure, and using the right fuel for your car can improve fuel efficiency.

Brake pads should generally be replaced every 30,000 to 50,000 miles, depending on driving habits and road conditions.

Check the battery, tires, windshield wipers, and antifreeze levels. Make sure the brakes are in good condition too.
